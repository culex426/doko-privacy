Privacy Policy

Welcome to Doko Document Organizer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Key Points:
  • All documents are stored locally on your device
  • We never access or transmit your documents to our servers
  • Optional Google Drive backup is controlled by you
  • We use Google AdMob for advertising

1. Information We Collect

Your Documents (Stored Locally)

  • Documents, images, and PDFs you upload or scan
  • Folder names, tags, and file organization data
  • All stored locally on your device with encryption

Automatically Collected

  • Device Info: Device model, OS version (for app compatibility)
  • Usage Data: App crashes and performance data (to improve the app)
  • Advertising Data: Collected by Google AdMob (see Section 3)

Biometric Data

If you enable fingerprint authentication, this is processed locally on your device. We never collect or transmit biometric data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Features: Document storage, organization, and scanning
  • Security: PIN and biometric protection for your documents
  • Cloud Backup (Optional): Sync with your Google Drive if you enable it
  • Improvements: Fix bugs and enhance performance

3. Third-Party Services

Google Services

  • Google Drive: Optional backup (only if you enable it)
  • Google ML Kit: Document scanning (processed locally on device)
  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements

Google Privacy Policy

Google AdMob Advertising

AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data.

Opt out of personalized ads:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

AdMob Privacy Policy

4. App Permissions

  • Camera: To scan documents
  • Storage: To save and access your documents
  • Internet: For Google Drive sync and ads
  • Biometric: For fingerprint authentication (optional)
  • Notifications: For reminders (optional)

5. Data Security

  • All documents stored locally with encryption
  • PIN and biometric authentication available
  • Secure folder for sensitive documents
  • Google Drive uses OAuth 2.0 secure authentication
  • We never transmit your documents to our servers

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Delete: Manage all documents within the app
  • Export: Share documents anytime
  • Control Backup: Enable/disable Google Drive sync in settings
  • Revoke Permissions: Change permissions in device settings
  • Uninstall: Removes all local data

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data:

  • When you explicitly share documents using the share feature
  • With Google (for Drive backup and ads, as described above)
  • If required by law or legal process

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Kept until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Google Drive: Managed by your Google account settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. You'll be notified of significant changes via in-app notification. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.
